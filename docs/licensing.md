# Licensing and Use Policy

The LUMINA Standard is intended to be open enough to spread, but controlled enough that the official source, name, marks, and certification remain protected.

> **Core principle:** The knowledge is open. The official name, source, and certification are protected.

This page explains what people may do with public LUMINA materials, what they must do when reusing them, and what requires written permission from Lumina Federation LLC.

!!! note "Plain-language summary"
    You may use, teach, print, and adapt public LUMINA reference materials with attribution under CC BY-SA 4.0. You may not remove attribution, rebrand LUMINA as your own system, or imply official certification, endorsement, or affiliation without permission.

## Public Materials License

Public-facing LUMINA Standard reference materials are made available under:

**Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International — CC BY-SA 4.0**

This applies to public educational materials such as:

- target diagrams
- translation sheets
- notation guides
- beginner handouts
- written explanations
- public instructional resources
- public curriculum excerpts

Under this license, people may use, share, print, teach from, and adapt these materials, provided they follow the attribution and ShareAlike requirements.

## What People May Do

People, schools, clubs, chapters, and instructors may:

- use public LUMINA materials for education
- teach from public LUMINA reference materials
- print LUMINA handouts for students, visitors, or workshops
- adapt the materials for local teaching needs
- create school-specific mapping sheets that translate their local system into LUMINA
- say that their materials are adapted from, compatible with, or based on the LUMINA Standard

## What People Must Do

Users of public LUMINA materials must:

- credit the LUMINA Standard
- retain the license notice
- link or refer back to the official LUMINA source when practical
- release adaptations under the same CC BY-SA 4.0 license
- avoid implying official certification, endorsement, or affiliation unless granted in writing by Lumina Federation LLC

Suggested attribution:

> Adapted from the LUMINA Standard, developed and maintained by Lumina Federation LLC. SaberCraft is the founding school/community of LUMINA.

## What Is Not Licensed Under Creative Commons

The Creative Commons license applies to public educational materials. It does **not** grant free use of brand identity, certification authority, or official endorsement language.

The following remain protected brand assets and are not freely licensed under Creative Commons:

- LUMINA
- LUMINA Standard
- LUMINA Notation
- SaberCraft
- Lumina Federation
- official logos
- certification seals
- instructor badges
- official endorsement language
- official curriculum titles where used as source identifiers

## Official Certification and Endorsement

Schools may teach from open LUMINA materials, but they may not claim to be official LUMINA schools, official LUMINA instructors, certified LUMINA instructors, approved training centers, or authorized representatives unless granted written permission by Lumina Federation LLC.

Acceptable language:

> Our school uses materials adapted from the LUMINA Standard.

> This worksheet maps our local numbering system to LUMINA target notation.

Not acceptable without written permission:

> We are an official Lumina school.

> We certify Lumina instructors.

> This is the official Lumina curriculum.

> Our version replaces the official LUMINA Standard.

## Why This Approach

The goal is not to lock LUMINA away. A standard only works if people can actually use it.

The goal is controlled openness:

1. public reference materials remain usable and shareable
2. attribution keeps the original source visible
3. adaptations remain open under the same license
4. official names, marks, badges, and certifications stay protected
5. the official version remains anchored to Lumina Federation LLC and the public LUMINA site

LUMINA should be easy to adopt honestly and difficult to rebrand dishonestly.

## Permission Requests

For official certification, logo use, commercial publication, formal school affiliation, or other uses not covered by this page, contact Lumina Federation LLC through the official SaberCraft or LUMINA channels.
